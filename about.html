AUTODRAG - Drone Club is one of the reputed club in the college

Recently they participated in a contest held in THAPAR INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY, named Saturnalia in which they have won the 1st prize.

This club provides a platform for students and enthusiasts to explore, design, build, program, and fly Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). The club environment fosters hands-on experience, teamwork, and innovation, often culminating in practical projects and competitions
